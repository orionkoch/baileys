Baileys® Coffee Creamers F.A.Q's

DO BAILEYS® COFFEE CREAMERS CONTAIN ALCOHOL, CAFFEINE, OR GLUTEN?

No, none of our coffee creamer products contain alcohol, caffeine, or gluten. So you don’t have to worry about catching a buzz of getting sick.

DOES IT NEED TO BE REFRIDGERATED?

That’s affirmative. Our creamers contain dairy cream components, so keep the chilled if you want to keep it tasting heavenly.

IF I AM LACTOSE-INTOLERANT IS IT OKAY FOR ME TO DRINK?

In short, no. Our product formula uses some dairy cream components, and while they aren’t a significant source of lactose, the product does contain dairy. So, these products are not appropriate for individuals with a milk allergy. Better safe than sorry, right?

THE INGREDIENTS CONTAIN CREAM SO WHY IS IT CONSIDERED A CREAMER?

True, we do use cream in our products but not in significant amounts. So technically, our products are classified as creamers. Just remember to steer clear if you have a milk allergy. After all, they do contain dairy.

IS THE BOTTLE RECYCLABLE?

You know it! Our bottles are made of recyclable plastic #1, and the caps are made of recyclable plastic #5. Every town is different, so check with your local recycling center to make sure that accept them. As for the small foil seal, kindly place that in the trash.

HOW LONG DOES IT STAY FRESH? BAILEYS®

Coffee Creamers are ultra pasteurized, giving them a longer shelf life when unopened. After you remove the freshness seal, they'll stay deliciously creamy for solid 14 days. Yum.
